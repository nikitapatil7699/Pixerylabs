About Pixery

Pixery is a global tech. company building innovative apps for next generation mobile creatives. Our video apps reach more than 100 million people, consistently top-ranked and featured by Apple and Google. Pixery has been consistently ranked within the fastest growing tech companies in Turkey and became one of the fastest growing tech companies in the EMEA region reported by Deloitte. For more information about our products visit ourwebsite

We are building a culture with hard work and humility at its core. We inspire one another by sharing information, knowledge, and ideas that create a transparent environment where everyone’s voice is heard. Our culture attracts passionate hearts and minds who always demonstrate originality, courage, curiosity, inclusion.

What you will be working on:
  • Manage and optimize performance marketing campaigns across platforms like Google Ads, Meta, Apple Search Ads, TikTok, and other advertising networks.
  • Collaborate closely with the creative team to guide creative asset production and testing based on performance data.
  • Conduct A/B and multivariate tests to continually refine and improve campaign performance.
  • Analyze and report on key metrics including CPI, CAC, LTV, ROI, and user funnel effectiveness to optimize user acquisition.
  • Oversee and direct App Store Optimization (ASO) efforts and manage relationships with external marketing agencies.
  • Stay up-to-date with the latest trends and technologies in digital marketing to keep ahead in the industry.
  • Drive product enhancements by leveraging data from performance marketing campaigns and maintaining oversight of product metrics

Essential Skills and Experience:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, Economics, Engineering or related fields.
  • Experience in digital marketing, preferably in the mobile apps sector.
  • Strong proficiency in managing digital advertising platforms such as Google Ads, Meta and TikTok.
  • Experience with measurement platforms like Firebase, AppsFlyer, and Google Analytics.
  • Good understanding of data analysis and interpretation.
  • Excellent communication skills in English, both written and spoken.
  • A creative, curious, and growth-driven mindset with an ability to work in a fast-paced, results-oriented environment.

What it’s like to work at Pixery?

  • Access to the worldwide community: working on products -you can be genuinely proud of- used by millions of people around the world.
  • Make a difference in the mobile video creativity market: At the intersection of innovation and technology, we are building something that inspires the mobile creativity market.

We believe that it takes a team to build something even bigger; if you have a love for technology and want to make a meaningful impact in the mobile video creativity industry, join us to inspire the next generation of creators and wow our millions of users every day!
