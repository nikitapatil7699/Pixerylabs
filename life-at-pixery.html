Hackathons, webinars and podcasts

We organized our first external hackathon in 2019. For 2 days, participants tried to facilitate their best prototypes in the fastest growing technologies, such as augmented reality, computer vision, machine learning, camera, and video. We all worked hard and had fun for those two days.

Not only external hackathons, but our internal hackathons are also fun and challenging. We regularly organize competitions to create new product features, new effects and edit challenges to use our products to its fullest capability.
